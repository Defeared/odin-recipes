Easy pizza recipe

Description

Hey there friends, if your looking for a fun, fast and easy to get some pizza you've come to the right place.

I know we've all been in a spot in our lives when pizza is just on your mind. In this short article, I'm going to show you how to get pizza down your throat hole with little to no effort at all.

What you need

  • Acess to the internet or phone
  • Money
  • Car (optional)
  • Time

How to get Pizza ordering online

  1. Open the Papa Johns app or search online for Papa Johns. You usually get better deals with online oder coupons
  2. I recommend you browse through deals and specails, unless you know exactly what you want.
  3. Add whatever you want listed on the menu
  4. Go to the checkout
  5. Select if you'd want pick up or delivery
  6. And boom your pizza will soon be ready, either to be picked up by you or delivered to your house

How to order on the phone

  1. Use a phone book to find the nearest Papa Johns
  2. Decide what items you want from the menu
  3. Use the phone number listed and give them a call
  4. Tell the workers what you would like
  5. Specify if you want pickup or delivery
  6. Figure out payment
  7. And boom your pizza will soon be ready, either to be picked up by you or delivered to your house
