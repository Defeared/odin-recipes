Ingredients

  • Meat: lean ground beef and itlain sausage (as much as you want :))
  • Onion and garlic: cook this stuff with the meat for a ticket to flavor town
  • Tomato stuff: use a can of crushed tomatos and 2 cans of tomato sauce, one can of paste.
    use what ever red sauce you want, it really doesnt matter, but always use tomato paste. Tomato paste added to anything with red sauce is great.
  • Sugar: two table spoons, this helps with acidity
  • Spices and seasoning: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles: Get them from the store, only try hards make homemade noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta
  • Egg: binds the ricotta to the noodles so it doesnt ooze out when cut

How to make lasagna step-by-step

here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make your lasagna

  1. Prepare Ingredients
  2. cook meat with onion and garlic (drain optional I guess)
  3. Add tomato stuff with seasoning and spices with water. Bring to a simmer and cover for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Cook noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 mins. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  5. Make ricotta mix with egg and some parsley and salt

Once you have everything cooked and ready follow these instructions

Building the Lasagna

  1. Preheat over to 375f (190c)
  2. Spread your meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish (9x13 inch dish is recommended)
  3. Add a layer of noodles
  4. Add meat sauce
  5. Add ricotta, followed with mozzarella and parmesan
  6. Add noodle layer
  7. Repeat as needed
  8. Top with mozzarella and parmesan

Baking and serving

  1. Cover pan with tin foil. To prevent from sticking, put cooking oil on tin foil or make sure it doesnt touche the cheese
  2. Place in a preheated 375f (190c) oven and bake for 25 mins. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 mins
  3. Remove and rest for 15 mins
  4. Serve
