Description

Howdy, I just want the world to know how to make the best tacos ever! In the article I'll show you step by step how to make tastey tacos and help you prove to the world you can do something right.

Its so easy, I learned how to make this by watching one time!

What you need

  • Taco sized tortillas flour or corn, hard or soft
  • Ground beef/ chicken
  • Your favorite taco seasoning
  • Onion
  • Tomatos
  • Shredded cheese of choice
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Sour Cream
  • Salsa(make is as spicy as you want)
  • (optional)Guacomle
  • (optional)Refried beans
  • (optional)Your favorite hot sauce/ sauce

Instructions

  1. Dice your onion and tomatos
  2. Cook your protien, (I usually cook my onoins with the meat, but you dont have to.)
  3. Add seasoning and follow directions on pouch
  4. Once meat has been seaoned and finished cooking, set to low heat to keep warm
  5. Stage the rest of your ingredients in bowls and serving dishes

Boom you have a nice taco assembly station for you and your family

Assembly Claytons way

  1. Lay your tortilla on a plate
  2. Add your cheese
  3. Meat
  4. Sauce of choice
  5. Vegie toppings
  6. Refried beans
  7. Guac if you got it
  8. Shredded lettuce
